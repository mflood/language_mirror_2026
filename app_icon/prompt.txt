Six Wands Studios — Visual Style Guide (for AI image prompts)

Studio: Six Wands Studios — independent iOS game studio. The name comes from I Ching Hexagram 1 (乾), The Creative: six unbroken yang lines, heaven over heaven.

Core Symbol: Six solid horizontal lines stacked vertically (the hexagram). Always six, always unbroken.

---

THE FEELING

Pop surrealism meets Eastern mysticism. Storybook-gothic. Beautiful but a little eerie — like something you found in an attic that you're not sure you were supposed to open. Cute with depth underneath. Playful on the surface, esoteric underneath.

Think: Mark Ryden, old grimoires, Victorian children's books, ukiyo-e woodblock prints, mist over pine trees, glowing sigils, whispered secrets, big-eyed doll-like figures holding arcane books, wands radiating in sunburst patterns.

Not: Corporate tech, neon gamer, flat/minimal SaaS, bright and cheerful, cartoon/Pixar, photorealistic.

---

COLOR PALETTE

- Black: #0a0a0c (deep, near-true black)
- White: #e8e4df (warm parchment, not clinical white)
- Red (primary): #8b2d3a (deep, desaturated, like dried blood or old velvet)
- Red deep: #5a1a28 (shadows, dark accents)
- Red light: #c44058 (highlights, glows)
- Cream: #d4cec4 (aged paper tone)
- Red glow: rgba(196, 64, 88, 0.25) (for soft luminous effects)

The palette is desaturated and moody. Reds are deep, not bright. Whites are warm parchment. Never: pure white, bright red, amber, gold, blue, neon.

---

TYPOGRAPHY FEEL

- Headings/titles: elegant serif (Cormorant Garamond) — literary, slightly old-world
- Body: light, airy sans-serif (Raleway weight 300) — modern and quiet
- The contrast between ornate serif and minimal sans is intentional

---

APP ICON GUIDELINES

- Should feel like a small mysterious artifact, not a generic app icon
- Desaturated palette with selective red or cream accents
- Can incorporate: hexagram lines, sigils, esoteric geometry, storybook imagery
- Rounded corners (iOS standard: 20% radius of icon size)
- Should read well at small sizes — keep compositions simple with one strong focal element
- Dark backgrounds preferred, with subject emerging from shadow

---

REFERENCE IMAGE

The studio's hero image depicts: a pale, big-eyed girl with white hair and red flower accents, wearing dark Victorian clothing, holding an open arcane book. A glowing hexagram floats before her. Six wands radiate outward in a sunburst behind her. Misty East Asian pine landscape in the background. Muted, nearly monochrome with selective warm accents. This is the target aesthetic.
